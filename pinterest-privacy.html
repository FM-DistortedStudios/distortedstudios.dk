01

Who we are

Distorted Studios is a Copenhagen-based studio that builds automated revenue systems for businesses. As part of our services, we connect to the Pinterest API to manage and automate Pinterest marketing on behalf of our clients.

For privacy-related enquiries: fm@distortedstudios.dk

02

What Pinterest data we access

When you connect your Pinterest account to our platform via Pinterest's official OAuth 2.0 authentication, we request access to:

  • Boards & Pins — to read, create, and manage your Pinterest boards and pins as part of your automated content strategy
  • Catalogs — to read and manage your product catalog for automated feed management and optimisation
  • Ads data — to read and manage your advertising campaigns, ad groups, creatives, and performance metrics
  • Account information — basic profile data such as your Pinterest username and account ID, used to identify your account within our system
  • Analytics — performance data for your pins, boards, and ads, used to generate reports and drive automated decisions

We request only the permissions necessary to deliver the services you have engaged us for.

03

How we use your Pinterest data

Your Pinterest data is used exclusively to:

  • Operate and manage automated revenue systems on your behalf
  • Schedule, publish, and manage pins and ad campaigns
  • Monitor and optimise catalog feeds and advertising performance
  • Generate reports and insights for your account
  • Improve the reliability and performance of our platform
We do not sell, rent, share, or use your Pinterest data for any purpose outside of delivering the services you have specifically requested. Your data is never used for our own marketing or advertising.
04

Authentication & token storage

We authenticate with Pinterest exclusively through their official OAuth 2.0 system. We never ask for or store your Pinterest password.

Access tokens and refresh tokens are:

  • Stored using industry-standard encryption
  • Transmitted only over secure HTTPS connections
  • Accessible only to authorised systems within our platform
  • Refreshed automatically using Pinterest's token refresh mechanism before expiry
05

Data sharing & third parties

We do not share your Pinterest data with any third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

Your data may be processed by our infrastructure providers (such as hosting and database services) solely for the purpose of operating our platform. All such providers are contractually bound to GDPR-compliant data processing terms.

06

Data retention & deletion

We retain Pinterest account data only for as long as you are an active client. Upon termination of our engagement or upon your request:

  • Your Pinterest access tokens are immediately revoked and deleted
  • All associated Pinterest data stored in our systems is deleted within 30 days
  • You can also revoke our access at any time directly via your Pinterest account security settings
07

Security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your Pinterest data, including:

  • Encrypted storage of all authentication credentials
  • HTTPS-only data transmission
  • Access controls limiting who within our team can access account data
  • Regular review of security practices and infrastructure
08

Your rights

You have the right at any time to:

  • Disconnect your Pinterest account from our platform
  • Request deletion of all Pinterest data we hold on your behalf
  • Request a summary of what Pinterest data we have stored
  • Revoke our API access directly through Pinterest's security settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at fm@distortedstudios.dk and we will respond within 5 business days.

09

Pinterest's own policies

Your use of Pinterest is also governed by Pinterest's own terms and privacy policies. We operate in accordance with the Pinterest Developer Guidelines and Pinterest's Privacy Policy.

10

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our services or Pinterest's platform requirements. Any updates will be reflected with a revised date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
